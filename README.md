# Business Introduction

NAS Chocolate is a multinational confectionery company specializing in premium milk and dark chocolate products distributed across multiple international markets. As global operations expanded, leadership required clear visibility into sales performance across countries and sales teams to support strategic growth, resource allocation, and performance management.

To address this need, NAS Chocolate commissioned the development of a comprehensive sales performance dashboard to consolidate key business metrics and provide actionable insights across its regions of operation.

---

# Problem Statement

Prior to this analysis, NAS Chocolate faced several reporting and decision-making challenges:

- Limited visibility into total global sales performance
- Lack of standardized reporting across operating countries
- Difficulty identifying top-performing sales personnel
- No centralized summary of shipments and distribution volume
- Inability to compare country-level contributions to overall revenue

Without an integrated view of these metrics, leadership could not effectively evaluate market performance or optimize sales strategy.

# Recommendations

## Market Strategy
- Invest further in Australia, the strongest revenue market.
- Explore growth initiatives in Canada and New Zealand to close performance gaps.
- Maintain diversified country presence to reduce geographic risk.

## Sales Team Optimization
- Replicate best practices from top performers across the salesforce.
- Implement performance incentives aligned with top-tier benchmarks.
- Provide targeted support to mid-tier performers to elevate productivity.

## Logistics & Distribution
- Maintain current shipment efficiency levels.
- Monitor shipment-to-box ratios to sustain cost-effective distribution.
- Evaluate regional shipment demand patterns for optimization.

## Executive Reporting
- Continue using the Tableau dashboard as a centralized performance tool.
- Introduce trend analysis over time to track growth trajectories.
- Add country-level salesperson breakdown for deeper insights.

# Conclusion

The NAS Chocolate sales analysis provides a comprehensive view of global business performance, highlighting strong revenue generation, efficient distribution, and a high-performing international sales team. Australia emerges as the leading market, while other regions maintain stable contributions, supporting diversified growth.

The Tableau dashboard successfully consolidates critical business metrics into a clear, interactive format, enabling NAS Chocolate leadership to make informed strategic decisions across markets, sales operations, and distribution planning.
